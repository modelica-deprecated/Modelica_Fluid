Modelica_Fluid.Pipes.BaseClasses.WallFriction.NoFriction

No pipe wall friction, no static head

Information




This component sets the pressure loss due to wall friction
to zero, i.e., it allows to switch off pipe wall friction.




Extends from PartialWallFriction (Partial wall friction characteristic (base package of all wall friction characteristics)).
